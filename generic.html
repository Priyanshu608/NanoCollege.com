NanoCollege

The Nano College will be a web-based system with a very user-friendly interface which indeed will make the whole management process easy to manage and operate with zero redundancies. A website that will be mainly designed for the students of SIES College pursuing their bachelor’s degree. This website will provide efficiency in the college life of a student by combining certain educational and user-friendly social components together

As far as the educational factors are concerned students will be able to find all the basic study materials like PDF’s, reference books, articles, link of the YouTube videos, etc. along with this they would also be able to connect through Chat box and group chats which will help them to interact and solve each other’s doubt. Also, there would be a social feed Available with personalized sections through which they could socialize, solve each other’s doubt on a large scale and be updated about the college events..
